About Us

"Aduaba Fresh" is inspired by the philosophy that living on healthy foods should never be a burden. It is an on-demand app where Fresh fruits, vegetables and healthy meals can be easily sourced and delivered to support a healthy lifestyle.

Aduaba Fresh is your one stop shop for all your fruits, vegetables and nutty needs, our promise is to always deliver these products to you fast, fresh and at the right price. Aduaba Fresh supports all your healthy lifestyle needs and also provides a host of resources to ensure you maintain that lifestyle.

Beyond these fresh produces, we also have an inventory of grab and go meals like salads, fresh wraps, fresh juices, soups, grilled chicken, grilled fish for our on-the-go customers. We use the city as your warehouse and we always ensure that we don't take a lot to deliver your items to you whenever you need them and in the right conditions.

Our services can be accessed by downloading the "Aduaba Fresh" mobile app on the Apple and Android play store.
